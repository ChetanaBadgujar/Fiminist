ABOUT US

Our Mission

Women for Women International helps the most poor and marginalized women in conflict and fighting nations earn and save money, improve their health and well-being, contribute to decisions in their families and communities, and connect to support networks. She is able to generate long-term change for herself, her family, and her community by applying her talents, knowledge, and resources.

Our Vision

What is the vision of women empowerment? We envision a world without poverty in which women are empowered, uplifted and equal partners in society. Women live in safe, sustainable environments that include adequate food, water, housing, education and health care.
